Gemma 3 27B is Google's open weights dense decoder. Notable architectural choices:
**QK-Norm** (RMSNorm applied to Q and K before the attention dot product), an aggressive **5:1
interleaving** of local sliding-window attention with global attention layers, and a "norm
everywhere" placement that adds a Post-Norm after each block in addition to the standard
Pre-Norm.

The interleaved SWA + global pattern is the defining long-context move: most layers see only a
local 4K window, with every 6th layer doing full attention at a much higher RoPE base.
