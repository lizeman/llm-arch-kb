Qwen3 30B-A3B (May 2025) is the small MoE variant of the Qwen 3 family — 30B total
parameters, 3B active per token. It carries the same architectural pattern as the
[235B-A22B flagship](/llm-arch-kb/models/qwen3-235b-a22b/) at a smaller scale: 48 layers,
32 query heads sharing 4 KV heads under [GQA](/llm-arch-kb/attention/gqa/), Pre-Norm
[RMSNorm](/llm-arch-kb/normalization/rmsnorm/), [SwiGLU](/llm-arch-kb/ffn-moe/swiglu/) FFN
inside each expert, [RoPE](/llm-arch-kb/positional/rope/) with ABF-scaled base, and
[QK-Norm](/llm-arch-kb/normalization/qk-norm/) in attention.

The MoE configuration is unchanged from the flagship: **128 experts, top-8 routing, no
shared expert**, global-batch load balancing. The intent — laid out in §2.1 of the technical
report — is that the same routing recipe can be carried across both MoE sizes without
retuning.

3B active parameters at competitive quality is the headline efficiency claim of the small
MoE: the model achieves quality that the technical report compares to the 32B dense at a
fraction of the per-token compute.
