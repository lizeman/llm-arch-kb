Attention is the part of the transformer that has changed most under inference pressure.
Each step on this path keeps the math recognizably "scaled dot product over heads" but trades a
little quality for a lot of KV-cache compression. The endpoint, in 2026, is either GQA
(most frontier dense decoders) or MLA (DeepSeek-line).
