Normalization is the cheapest module in a transformer block and one of the most argued-about.
This short path takes you from the original LayerNorm to RMSNorm, surveys where the norm
goes (pre, post, sandwich), and finishes with QK-Norm — the small addition that has slipped
into several recent frontier dense decoders.
